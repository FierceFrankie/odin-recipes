Cream Cheese Chicken Enchiladas

Description:

This easy recipe is a family favorite. Everyone will be coming back for seconds!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of shredded chicken breast
  • tortilla shells
  • cream cheese
  • cream of chicken
  • chopped celery
  • shredded cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil and season chicken breast. then shred up the chicken breast.
  2. Layer tortillas with cream cheese. Let the cream cheese sit out of the fridge for a few minutes to make it easier to spread.
  3. Mix shredded chicken with chopped celery, cream of chicken, and shredded cheese.
  4. Scoop mixture into tortilla shells wrap up the tortillas, layer with cheese in a casserole dish, and bake for 20 minutes.
