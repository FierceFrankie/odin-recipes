Beef Enchilada Casserole

Description:

This recipe is a great twist on taco night.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • shredded cheese
  • flour tortillas
  • olives
  • diced onion
  • chopped celery
  • enchilada sauce

Steps:

  1. Chop up onions and celery. Prepare the ingredients to mix with the meat.
  2. Mix ingredients with hamburger and brown the meat. Then add in the taco seasoning and water.
  3. Prepare a frying pan and pour in enchilada sauce.
  4. Warm up the flour tortillas in the pan with enchilada sauce. Then place the tortillas in the casserole dish.
  5. Layer with meat and veggie mixture and cheese and then add another layer of tortilla shells and repeat.
